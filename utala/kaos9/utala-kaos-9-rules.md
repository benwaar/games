# Utala: KAOS 9

*A 2-player tactical grid combat card game.*

---

# Objective

Be the first player to achieve **3 Rocketmen wins in a row**  
(horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).

If no player achieves 3-in-a-row after all combat resolves,  
the player controlling the **most squares** wins.

# Phase 1: Placement

Players alternate turns placing one Rocketman on any empty square.

Rules:
- 2, 3, 9, 10 must be placed face-down.
- A square may contain one Rocketman from each player (contested).
- No combat occurs during placement.
- Continue until all 9 squares are filled.

---

# Phase 2: Dogfights
